Zombies on Campus

Zombies on Campus is a fun, quick choice-based game. It was built as a project for 2016 Internet Technology course, taught by Leif Azzopardi.

The team that built this game:

  • Konstantinos Kagiampakis
  • Shallu Gangwani
  • Simonas Viliunas
  • Laura Zebrauskaite

The project was built using a variety of technologies: Django Framework, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, AJAX - with a help of a Leif Azzopardi's book Tango with Django.
